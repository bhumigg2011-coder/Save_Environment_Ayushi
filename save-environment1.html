Environmental Pollution

Environmental pollution is the contamination of air, water, and soil by harmful substances. Human activities such as industrialization, deforestation, and excessive use of fossil fuels are the main causes of environmental pollution.

Importance of Protecting the Environment

  • Maintains ecological balance
  • Protects human health
  • Preserves biodiversity
  • Ensures a safe future for coming generations

Causes of Environmental Pollution

CAUSES OF POLLUTION
  • Industrial smoke and chemical waste
  • Vehicular emissions
  • Plastic waste and improper disposal
  • Deforestation and urbanization
  • Overuse of fertilizers and pesticides

Effects of Pollution on Earth

  1. Global warming and climate change
  2. Increase in respiratory and water-borne diseases
  3. Loss of wildlife and biodiversity
  4. Soil infertility and food scarcity

Types of Pollution

Type Causes Harmful Effects
Air Vehicle and factory smoke Asthma and lung diseases
Water Sewage and industrial waste Water contamination and diseases
Soil Chemicals and plastic waste Reduced soil fertility
Noise Traffic and loud machines Stress and hearing problems
